## 2. Platform Priority

### Tier 1: TikTok
- **Why:** Largest freelancer audience in target markets (NG, EG). Short-form video dominates discovery for the 18-35 demographic Cenoa serves.
- **Market fit:** Nigeria is Cenoa's #1 Deposit market (45.2% of deposits) and #2 Get Paid market (35.1%). TikTok over-indexes in NG.
- **Caution:** Paid TikTok failed (0 withdrawals). But paid ≠ organic+boosted. CPI campaigns target different audiences than boosted organic content.

### Tier 2: Instagram
- **Why:** Visual storytelling (fee comparisons, testimonials, UI demos). Strong in Turkey — Cenoa's #1 market by feature engagement (TR = 46–61% of all feature usage).
- **Current state:** 2 installs/week. Low bar to beat.

### Tier 3: Twitter/X
- **Why:** Fintech community, crypto-adjacent audience, freelancer thought leadership.
- **Best for:** Brand awareness and community (not direct installs). Lower priority for paid boost.

## 3. Content Types That Convert for Fintech

Each content type maps to a specific feature engagement insight:

### A) "Payoneer Fee Comparison" Infographic → Boost
- **Hook:** Side-by-side: Payoneer/Wise fees vs Cenoa for $1,000 payment
- **Why it works:** Get Paid is the #1 feature (6,872 events/week). Users already care about receiving money — show them the savings.
- **Format:** Static carousel (IG), short video (TikTok)
- **Target:** TR (Get Paid 46.9%), NG (Get Paid 35.1%)

### B) "How I Earn in USD" Testimonial → Boost
- **Hook:** Real freelancer showing their Cenoa balance, talking about receiving payments
- **Why it works:** Trust + social proof. NG freelancers on Upwork/Fiverr need relatable voices.
- **Format:** 30-60s UGC-style video (TikTok, IG Reels)
- **Target:** NG (Deposit #1 at 45.2%), EG (card interest 38% of feature mix)

### C) "3-Minute Account Opening" Demo → Boost
- **Hook:** Screen recording showing how fast Cenoa onboarding is
- **Why it works:** Current install→signup rate is only 12-16% for most channels. Showing the simplicity pre-install reduces friction.
- **Format:** Screen recording with voiceover (TikTok, IG Reels)
- **Target:** All markets

### D) "Debit Card Unboxing / First Tap" → Boost (TR & EG)
- **Hook:** Physical card arrival, first contactless payment
- **Why it works:** Debit Card is the #1 feature in TR (31.3% of feature mix) and EG (38.0% of feature mix). Card content will resonate.
- **Format:** 15-30s video (IG Stories, TikTok)
- **Target:** TR, EG

## 5. Boost Criteria — What Gets Boosted

**Rule: Only boost organic winners.**

| Metric | Threshold | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Engagement rate | >2× account average | Proven resonance before spend |
| Comments | >5 genuine comments | Signal of conversation, not just likes |
| Saves/shares | >1.5× average | High-intent actions predict install intent |
| Content type | Must be one of the 4 types above | Stay on-strategy |

**Process:**
1. Post organically (3-5x/week across platforms)
2. After 24-48 hours, evaluate against thresholds
3. Top 2-3 posts per week get boosted ($25-50 each)
4. Never boost content that didn't prove itself organically first

## 8. ⚠️ CRITICAL CAVEAT: Attribution Gap

### The Problem:
Current data shows **986 unattributed sign-ups** and **1,355 unattributed withdrawals** per week (68% of all sign-ups, 75% of all withdrawals are "(none)" in AppsFlyer).

**What this means for social boosts:**
- A user sees a boosted TikTok → visits the App Store directly → installs → attributed as **Organic**
- A user clicks an IG bio link → lands on cenoa.com → downloads app → attributed as **cenoa.com** or **(none)**
- Web-to-app flows lose attribution at the browser→store handoff

### Impact on This Plan:
- **Boosted social traffic will likely show as "organic" installs** — inflating organic numbers while social appears to underperform
- At $500/mo, the install volume will be small enough that it's hard to separate signal from noise
- TikTok paid already showed 49 installs / 0 withdrawals — but some of those withdrawals may be hiding in the "(none)" bucket

### Mitigation:
1. **Use AppsFlyer OneLinks** (not generic app store links) on all boosted posts — this is the single most important tracking fix
2. **Deferred deep links** to capture web→app journeys
3. **Pre/post analysis:** Compare organic install volume in target countries during boost-on vs boost-off weeks
4. **Promo codes:** Unique codes per platform (e.g., "TIKTOK50" for sign-up bonus) to track attribution outside AppsFlyer
5. **Ask during onboarding:** "How did you hear about Cenoa?" — low-tech but effective at $500/mo scale
