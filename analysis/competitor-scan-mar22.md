## 4) Angles/Hooks We Should Counter

### 🔴 URGENT — Payoneer Stablecoin Push
- **Threat level:** HIGH
- **What:** Payoneer is actively running stablecoin waitlist ads globally (EN + ES). "Money moving 24/7" narrative directly overlaps Cenoa's value prop.
- **Counter:** Cenoa needs to **own the stablecoin savings narrative** before Payoneer captures it. Differentiate on: (a) savings/yield angle Payoneer doesn't offer, (b) simplicity for individuals vs Payoneer's B2B focus, (c) emerging market focus vs Payoneer's global SMB approach.

### 🟡 Wise Transparency Moat
- **Threat level:** MEDIUM
- **What:** Wise's "see the real cost" and mid-market rate comparisons are deeply embedded in their brand. Hard to compete on transparency when they've been doing it for years.
- **Counter:** Don't compete on transparency alone — **compete on outcome** ("keep more of your money" vs "see the fees"). Cenoa's stablecoin angle means fees are structurally lower, not just transparently shown.

### 🟡 Wise Comedy / Entertainment Creative
- **Threat level:** MEDIUM
- **What:** Wise's TV campaign uses humor to make money transfer relatable and shareable. High production value.
- **Counter:** Cenoa can't outspend on TV. But can outperform on **social-native content** — shorter, more authentic, more relevant to emerging market audiences. Wise's humor skews very North American.

### 🟢 Payoneer's B2B / Checkout Angle
- **Threat level:** LOW (for Cenoa's B2C focus)
- **What:** Payoneer is investing heavily in B2B checkout / e-commerce seller tools.
- **Counter:** This is a different ICP than Cenoa's. But watch for spillover into freelancer payments — Payoneer's traditional stronghold.

### 🟢 Payoneer Localized LATAM/APAC Push
- **Threat level:** MEDIUM
- **What:** Payoneer running localized campaigns in PT (Brazil), ES (LATAM), VI (Vietnam). Shows investment in emerging market expansion.
- **Counter:** Cenoa should ensure TR/NG/EG campaigns are equally localized. Don't just translate — **culturally adapt**. Payoneer is doing this well.

## 5) Gaps We Can Exploit

### Gap 1: Neither competitor owns "stablecoin for individuals" in emerging markets
- Payoneer's stablecoin push is B2B-focused (business accounts, cross-border trade)
- Wise doesn't mention stablecoin/crypto at all in their ads
- **Opportunity:** Cenoa can own the "stablecoin savings for regular people" positioning in TR/NG/EG before either competitor moves there

### Gap 2: No competitor runs compelling TR/NG/EG-specific creative
- Payoneer's localized ads focus on APAC (Vietnam) and LATAM (Brazil, Spanish markets)
- Wise's major campaigns target US/Canada and European corridors
- **Opportunity:** Cenoa has first-mover advantage in Turkey, Nigeria, Egypt for stablecoin savings messaging

### Gap 3: Wise's humor doesn't translate to emerging markets
- "Be Smart and Get Wise" campaign is very American/Canadian in humor style
- Comedy spots are culturally specific (NFL, SNL audience)
- **Opportunity:** Cenoa can create culturally resonant, locally-produced content that Wise can't easily replicate

### Gap 4: Neither competitor talks about "protecting savings from inflation"
- Payoneer talks about business efficiency and profit margins
- Wise talks about fees and exchange rates
- Neither addresses **purchasing power protection** — the core concern in TR/NG/EG
- **Opportunity:** "Protect your money from inflation" / "Save in USD" is an uncontested angle in paid ads

### Gap 5: Lead gen / whitepaper format is underused in consumer fintech
- Payoneer uses whitepapers but only for B2B
- Wise doesn't do lead gen content ads
- **Opportunity:** "Free guide: How Turks are protecting their savings in 2026" could work as a top-of-funnel lead gen format

### Gap 6: No competitor uses "real people / real stories" UGC from emerging markets
- Neither Payoneer nor Wise features authentic user stories from Turkey, Nigeria, or Egypt
- **Opportunity:** User testimonial UGC from actual Cenoa users in these markets would be highly differentiated and trustworthy
