# Cenoa vs Payoneer: Fee & Feature Comparison

> **Purpose:** Feed into LP copy, ad creatives, SEO comparison articles, and social proof content.
> **Last updated:** 2026-03-22
> **Sources:** payoneer.com/about/pricing, payfeecalculator.com, wise.com/us/blog, xflowpay.com

## 1. Payoneer Fee Breakdown (from official sources)

### Receiving Fees
- **From marketplaces/clients (Global Payment Service):** ~1% for USD payments, up to 3% for non-USD
- **From another Payoneer user:** Free or up to 1%
- **Via credit card payment request:** Up to 3%

### Withdrawal Fees
- **To local bank (same currency):** ~$1.50 flat fee
- **To local bank (different currency):** Up to 2% FX markup on mid-market rate + possible flat fee

### FX Conversion
- **Currency conversion markup:** Up to 2% above mid-market rate
- This is applied on TOP of the receiving fee — so total cost can reach ~3%

### Other Fees
- **Monthly fee:** None for basic account
- **Annual card fee:** Applies if activity threshold not met
- **Inactivity fee:** None (unlike PayPal's $29.95)

### Payoneer Total Cost Example ($1,000 USD → local currency)
- Receiving fee: $10 (1%)
- FX markup: $20 (2%)
- **Total lost: ~$30 per $1,000 (3%)**

## 2. Cenoa Fee Structure

- **Total fee:** <1% all-in (FX + transfer combined)
- **Monthly fee:** $0 — none
- **Account opening:** ~3 minutes, fully digital
- **Infrastructure:** Stripe (payments) + Lead Bank (US banking partner, FDIC member)
- **FX rate:** Near mid-market, transparent
- **Receiving from US clients:** Direct USD account via Stripe
- **KYC:** Streamlined digital process, no lengthy document review cycles

## 3. Side-by-Side Comparison

### Withdrawal to Local Bank
- **Payoneer:** Up to 2% FX markup + $1.50 flat fee per withdrawal
- **Cenoa:** <1% total fee, no flat fee — what you see is what you get

### Receiving from US Clients
- **Payoneer:** ~1% receiving fee on incoming USD payments via Global Payment Service
- **Cenoa:** Direct USD account powered by Stripe — no receiving fee on incoming transfers

### FX Conversion Rate
- **Payoneer:** Up to 2% markup above mid-market rate (stacks on top of receiving fee)
- **Cenoa:** <1% total spread — near mid-market rate, no hidden stacking of fees

### Monthly / Annual Fees
- **Payoneer:** No monthly fee, but annual card fee if activity threshold not met
- **Cenoa:** $0 — no monthly fee, no annual fee, no card fee, no minimum activity requirement

### Account Opening Speed
- **Payoneer:** Online registration, but verification can take 1–3 business days for full approval
- **Cenoa:** ~3 minutes end-to-end — open account, verify, start receiving same day

### KYC Process
- **Payoneer:** Multi-step verification, document uploads, may require additional review rounds
- **Cenoa:** Streamlined digital KYC powered by Stripe Identity — fast, minimal friction

## 5. Trust & Credibility Comparison

### Payoneer
- **Founded:** 2005, publicly traded (NASDAQ: PAYO)
- **Regulated:** Licensed as money transmitter in US states; EU e-money institution
- **Scale:** 5M+ customers, 190+ countries, 70 currencies
- **Banking partners:** Various global banks
- **Reputation:** Established brand, strong marketplace integrations (Amazon, Upwork, Fiverr)
- **Trustpilot:** Actively reviewed, mixed ratings (common for payment platforms at scale)

### Cenoa
- **Infrastructure:** Built on Stripe (largest private fintech, $65B+ valuation) + Lead Bank (FDIC-insured US bank)
- **Regulatory:** Funds held at FDIC-insured institution via Lead Bank partnership
- **Advantage:** Leverages Stripe's best-in-class compliance, fraud detection, and payment rails
- **Target:** Purpose-built for freelancers and remote workers in emerging markets (Turkey, LATAM, etc.)
- **Differentiator:** Modern architecture = lower fees, faster processing, better UX
- **Trade-off:** Newer brand — less name recognition, but institutional-grade backing

- "Your money is held at an FDIC-insured US bank (Lead Bank) and processed through Stripe — the same infrastructure trusted by Amazon, Google, and Shopify"
- "Cenoa combines startup speed with enterprise-grade security"
- "Payoneer built the category. Cenoa is reimagining it for the next generation of global workers."
