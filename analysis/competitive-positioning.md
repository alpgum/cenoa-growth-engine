# Competitive Positioning — Cenoa vs Payoneer (Performance + SEO)

**Purpose:** Create a clear, repeatable positioning for ads + landing pages + SEO pages targeting **“payoneer alternative”** and **“payoneer vs cenoa”** intent.

**Inputs (known facts):**
- Cenoa is positioned as **~10x cheaper vs Payoneer** in typical use cases.
- **Fees below 1%** (position as “under 1%” / “<1%” where compliant).
- Infrastructure: **Stripe + Lead Bank**.
- **Account opening ~3 minutes**.

---

## 1) Positioning statement (1-liner)
**Cenoa is the Payoneer alternative for freelancers who want to get paid globally with fees under 1% and open an account in minutes (powered by Stripe + Lead Bank infrastructure).**

Shorter ad-ready version:
- **“Payoneer’a alternatif: <1% ücret + 3 dakikada hesap.”**

---

## 2) 3 core pillars + proof points

### Pillar A — Cost clarity (save money vs Payoneer)
What we mean:
- Make the default mental model: **Payoneer = expensive/opaque**, **Cenoa = cheaper + clearer**.

Proof points / ways to substantiate (use on LP + ads):
- **“Ücretler %1’in altında”** (always pair with link/footnote to pricing details).
- **“Payoneer’a göre birçok senaryoda ~10x daha ucuz”** (use “up to / typical” language, define example amount).
- “Hidden fees” callout framed as education: *“Bazı sağlayıcılar ücretleri birden fazla kalemde yansıtır (transfer ücreti + kur makası + çekim ücreti).”*

Recommended proof artifacts:
- A **simple fee example** (e.g., $100 / $500 / $1,000) with assumptions.
- A **‘fees explained’** FAQ section (what we charge, what we don’t).


### Pillar B — Trust & infrastructure (Stripe + Lead Bank)
What we mean:
- Reduce “is this legit?” anxiety with recognizable rails.

Proof points / ways to substantiate:
- “Powered by **Stripe** payments infrastructure.”
- “Banking partner infrastructure via **Lead Bank**.”
- Trust stack above the fold:
  - clear partner logos (only if allowed)
  - privacy/security statements
  - support/contact clarity

Trust copy suggestions (compliance-safe):
- “Ödemeler ve altyapı süreçleri Stripe ve Lead Bank iş ortakları üzerinden yürütülür.”
- “KYC/uyumluluk kontrolleri kullanıcı güvenliği için uygulanır.”


### Pillar C — Speed to value (minutes, not days)
What we mean:
- The moment of truth is: *“I can open it now and start receiving.”*

Proof points / ways to substantiate:
- “Hesap açılışı **yaklaşık 3 dakika**.”
- Onboarding UX: fewer steps, clear KYC expectations.
- CTA for intent pages:
  - “Create account”
  - “Start receiving”
  - “Get paid globally”

## 3) Comparison table (Cenoa vs Payoneer) — bullets only (no markdown tables)

Use this structure on LPs and SEO pages. Keep language factual and avoid absolutes.

### Fees / cost
- **Cenoa:** Fees positioned as **under 1%** (with pricing link + examples).
- **Payoneer:** Fees vary by corridor and method; can be perceived as high when multiple fee components apply.

### Value proposition
- **Cenoa:** “Payoneer alternative” optimized for **cost + speed**.
- **Payoneer:** Broad global payouts network; known brand in freelancer payouts.

### Sign-up speed
- **Cenoa:** Account opening **~3 minutes** (may vary by verification).
- **Payoneer:** Onboarding can be longer depending on review/verification.

### Trust / infrastructure
- **Cenoa:** Powered by **Stripe + Lead Bank** infrastructure (state accurately).
- **Payoneer:** Established global fintech brand (strong recognition).

### Pricing transparency
- **Cenoa:** Lead with *one clear promise* (fees under 1%) + show example calculations.
- **Payoneer:** Users often report confusion due to different fee types (transfer, FX margin, withdrawal, etc.).

### Best-fit user (positioning)
- **Cenoa:** Freelancers who want a **cheaper** and **faster** way to get paid.
- **Payoneer:** Users who prioritize brand familiarity and existing platform integrations.

### “Payoneer alternative” intent match (SEO)
- **Cenoa:** Directly addresses “alternative” pain: save money + quick account.
- **Payoneer:** Not applicable (incumbent brand).

## 4) Objection handling (ad + LP ready)

### Objection: “Can I trust Cenoa? Is it regulated?”
What they’re really asking: *“Will my money be safe, and is this legit?”*

Response framework:
- Lead with infrastructure + process clarity (not vague claims).
- Explain KYC/AML as protection.

Copy options:
- “Cenoa, Stripe ve Lead Bank altyapı ortaklarıyla çalışır. Kimlik doğrulama ve uyumluluk kontrolleri kullanıcı güvenliği için yapılır.”
- “Güvenlik ve uyumluluk: KYC süreci + şeffaf ücret politikası + destek kanalları.”

Do not do:
- “Fully regulated everywhere” / “bank-level guarantee” unless legally true and documented.


### Objection: “Is Cenoa available in my country / can I use it with my platform?”
Response framework:
- Be explicit: availability depends on country + verification.
- Give next step: check eligibility, join waitlist if needed.

Copy options:
- “Ülke uygunluğu ve doğrulama adımlarına göre değişebilir. Uygulamadan dakikalar içinde kontrol edebilirsiniz.”
- “Bulunduğunuz ülke desteklenmiyorsa bekleme listesine katılın; açıldığında haber verelim.”


### Objection: “How do fees really work? Any hidden fees?”
Response framework:
- Explain with examples.
- Disclose assumptions.

Copy options:
- “Ücretlerimiz %1’in altında olacak şekilde tasarlanır. İşlem türüne göre değişebileceği için örnek hesaplamaları ve fiyat sayfasını inceleyin.”
- “Şeffaflık: tek sayfada ücretler + örnek transfer senaryoları.”


### Objection: “Is it actually faster than Payoneer?”
Response framework:
- Claim speed as *time to create account*, not instant payout everywhere.

Copy options:
- “Hesap açılışı genelde ~3 dakika. Doğrulama süresi ve ödeme yöntemine göre değişebilir.”
- “Hız: kayıt + doğrulama akışı optimize.”

## 6) Disallowed claims / compliance-safe phrasing

### Avoid / disallowed (unless legally verified + documented)
- “Cheapest in the world / guaranteed cheapest”
- “0 fees always”
- “Instant transfers everywhere”
- “Regulated in all countries”
- “Bank” claims if Cenoa is not itself a bank
- “FDIC insured” or any deposit insurance claim unless explicitly true for the product structure and presented with exact legal wording
- Naming Payoneer fees as a fixed number unless sourced and kept current

### Safer phrasing templates
- Comparative pricing:
  - “Birçok senaryoda Payoneer’a göre **daha düşük** ücret”
  - “**Tipik kullanımda** Payoneer’a göre **~10x’e kadar** daha ucuz (örnek senaryoya göre)”
- Fee promise:
  - “Ücretler **%1’in altında olacak şekilde** (işlem türüne göre değişebilir)”
- Speed:
  - “Hesap açılışı **yaklaşık 3 dakika** (doğrulama süresine göre değişebilir)”
- Trust:
  - “Stripe ve Lead Bank altyapı ortaklarıyla çalışır”

### Required footnotes / disclosures (recommended)
- “Ücretler işlem türüne, tutara ve yönteme göre değişebilir. Detaylar: fiyatlandırma sayfası.”
- “Karşılaştırma örnekleri belirli varsayımlara dayanır (tutar, yöntem, ülke).”

## 7) How to measure whether positioning lands (GA4 + Amplitude)

Primary question: Do “Payoneer alternative / vs” visitors **understand the value (cost + speed + trust)** and convert better?

### A) GA4 (web) — recommended custom events
Key funnel (comparison LP → app install):
- `view_payoneer_comparison_page`
- `click_pricing_link`
- `click_fee_example_expand`
- `click_trust_partner_expand`
- `click_cta_download`
- `click_cta_open_account`
- `start_signup_web` (if web signup exists)

Engagement quality:
- `scroll_50` / `scroll_90`
- `faq_open_fee` / `faq_open_trust` / `faq_open_availability`

Recommended GA4 parameters:
- `geo` (TR/NG/EG)
- `language`
- `landing_variant` (A/B)
- `claim_variant` (e.g., “<1%” vs “save more”)

Success metrics:
- LP → CTA click rate
- CTA click → store click-through rate
- Scroll depth + FAQ opens (signal that objections are addressed)


### B) Amplitude (app) — events to tie back to intent
Acquisition + activation events:
- `install_attributed_payoneer_intent` (derived from UTM/referrer)
- `signup_start`
- `signup_complete`
- `kyc_start`
- `kyc_complete`
- `account_opened`
- `first_receive_money_initiated`
- `first_receive_money_completed`
- `first_withdrawal_initiated`
- `first_withdrawal_completed`

Recommended Amplitude properties:
- `utm_source`, `utm_campaign`, `utm_content`
- `intent_cluster` = `payoneer_alternative` | `payoneer_vs_cenoa`
- `geo` = TR | NG | EG
- `pricing_claim_shown` (boolean)
- `trust_claim_shown` (boolean)
- `speed_claim_shown` (boolean)

Evaluation (how we know it lands):
- Higher `signup_complete` rate for intent clusters
- Lower drop-off between `signup_complete` → `kyc_complete`
- Faster time-to-first-value (time from install → `account_opened` / `first_receive_money_completed`)
- Retention proxy: D7 returning users for payoneer-intent cohorts


### C) Experiment design (practical)
Run 2 variants on “Payoneer alternative” LP:
- Variant 1: lead with **<1% fees** (cost-first)
- Variant 2: lead with **3-minute setup + trusted infrastructure** (trust/speed-first)

Declare winner by:
- Primary: `click_cta_download` and downstream `signup_complete`
- Secondary: `faq_open_*` distribution (what objections persist)
