## 6. learn.cenoa.com Optimization

### 6.1 Internal Linking Strategy

**Current problem:** Blog posts are isolated — no cross-linking, no topic clusters, no pillar page structure.

**Fix:**
- Create a **pillar page** at learn.cenoa.com: "The Complete Guide to Getting Paid as an International Freelancer"
- Every new article links back to the pillar page
- Every article links to 2–3 related articles
- Add contextual links to Cenoa product pages (cenoa.com/pricing, cenoa.com/how-it-works)

**Internal linking rules:**
- Every article must contain 3–5 internal links minimum
- Top-performing pages (`/blog/accepted-proof-of-payment`) should link to newer content
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")

### 6.2 CTA Placement

**Current problem:** CTAs are weak or missing on most blog posts.

**Optimized CTA strategy:**
| Position | CTA Type | Copy |
|---|---|---|
| After intro (paragraph 2) | Inline text CTA | "Skip the research — [try Cenoa free](link) and start receiving USD today" |
| Mid-article | Banner CTA | "💡 Cenoa gives you a US bank account in 3 minutes. [Get started →](link)" |
| End of article | Full CTA block | App store badges + "Join 50,000+ freelancers" social proof |
| Floating (mobile) | Sticky bottom bar | "Open your US account free" + download button |

### 6.3 Mobile UX Improvements

| Issue | Fix | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Slow load time on 3G (NG/EG users) | Compress images, lazy-load below fold | 🔴 Critical |
| No sticky CTA on mobile | Add floating bottom bar with app download | 🔴 Critical |
| Text too small on mobile | Increase body font to 16px minimum | 🟡 Medium |
| No breadcrumbs | Add breadcrumb nav for SEO + UX | 🟡 Medium |
| No table of contents | Add sticky TOC for long articles | 🟢 Nice-to-have |

### 6.4 Domain Strategy Decision

**Recommendation:** Consolidate blog content on cenoa.com/blog (main domain) over time.

**Why:**
- learn.cenoa.com as a subdomain doesn't pass full SEO authority to cenoa.com
- Google treats subdomains as semi-separate entities
- Competitors (Wise, Payoneer) all blog on their main domain

**Migration plan (not urgent — do after 90-day content push):**
1. Publish all new content on cenoa.com/blog
2. 301-redirect top learn.cenoa.com posts to cenoa.com/blog equivalents
3. Maintain learn.cenoa.com for educational/non-SEO content only
